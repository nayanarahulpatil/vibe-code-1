# KPI Document
## Project: Enterprise Employee Travel & Expense Management System

# 1. Problem Overview

## Problem Title
**Digital Transformation of Enterprise Travel & Expense Management Process**

## Background
The organization employs over 10,000 employees across multiple locations. Employees regularly travel for client engagements, audits, training programs, conferences, internal meetings, and other business activities.

Currently, the travel and expense management process relies on manual methods such as:
- Email-based travel requests and approvals
- Excel spreadsheets for expense tracking
- Phone calls for follow-ups
- Paper-based receipts and reimbursement documentation

The lack of a centralized system results in operational inefficiencies, delayed approvals, poor visibility, compliance risks, and employee dissatisfaction.

## Current Challenges

| Area | Current Challenge |
|--------|------------------|
| Travel Requests | Manual submission and tracking |
| Approvals | Delayed approvals due to email dependency |
| Expense Claims | Manual verification and reconciliation |
| Reimbursements | Long reimbursement cycle times |
| Compliance | Difficulty enforcing travel policies |
| Visibility | No centralized dashboard or status tracking |
| Reporting | Time-consuming manual reporting |
| Audit Readiness | Missing documentation and incomplete audit trails |
| Employee Experience | Frustrating and non-transparent process |

## Impact on Business & Users

### Business Impact
- Increased administrative cost
- Reduced operational efficiency
- Higher compliance risk
- Limited spend visibility
- Poor budget control
- Audit challenges

### Employee Impact
- Delayed travel approvals
- Slow reimbursements
- Low process transparency
- Increased manual effort
- Reduced employee satisfaction

# 2. Goal Definition

## Primary Objective
Implement a centralized Travel & Expense Management System that automates travel requests, approvals, expense claims, policy compliance, and reimbursement workflows.

## Secondary Objectives
1. Reduce travel request processing time.
2. Improve reimbursement turnaround time.
3. Increase policy compliance.
4. Improve employee satisfaction.
5. Increase operational efficiency.
6. Enhance spend visibility and reporting.
7. Improve audit readiness.
